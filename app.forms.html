validate_email(email)[kaynak]¶

Validates email to ensure it is not already registered in the database.

This method checks if the provided email is different from the current user’s email and verifies if the email has already been registered by another user. If the email is found in the database, a validation error is raised.

Parameters: email: The email address to be validated. Must be a valid email string.

Raises: ValidationError: Raised if the given email address is already registered by another user.
